Horror Movie Wednesdays

One of the "rituals" with two of my friends and I had was watching a bunch of horror movies together on Wednesdays. It was always me and my friends and Owen and Jared that would get together. Sadly, we are split very apart at college, but we still have found a way to make it work but streaming over Discord. So, now every Wednesday at 7 EST we watch another movie or two over Discord.

What have we seen?

We’ve seen classics like The Blair Witch Project, Friday the 13th, and Scream. We’ve seen new releases including Longlegs and The Last Straw. We’ve also ventured to the depths of Netflix to find obscure films such as Old People and The Ritual.
